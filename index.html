about us

We are working since 2015 for food,water,education , and other

At [Shree Ram Chandra Manav Kalyan Samiti], we have been dedicated to improving the lives of underprivileged individuals since our establishment in 2015. Our primary focus is on addressing the basic needs of food, water, and education for beggars and marginalized communities.

Our Mission:

Our mission is to create a more equitable society by providing essential support to those who are most vulnerable and often overlooked. We believe that every individual, regardless of their circumstances, deserves access to fundamental human rights such as food, clean water, and education.
